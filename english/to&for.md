# To VS For

To --> transfer / exchange 
事物的转移，交换

For --> benefit = good
好的事物，对人有好处，有好处

例句:

1. I made this lesson (?) you. **for**

2. I gave the beer (?) you.  **to**    
    exchange beer

3. I'm going to talk (?) you. **to**   
    Exchange info

4. I will do that (?) you. **for** 

5. I sing (?) you. **to** X  true:for

6. I go (?) school. **to**   
    transfer body
