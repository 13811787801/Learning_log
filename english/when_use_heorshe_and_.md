# When use he / she or her /his

he is boy

she is girl
## he / she 

### Grammar

subject + **verb** (+ noun)  *ps: must follow by a verb.*

#### example

1. he / she  + verb.

2. he like

3. she hates boy

4. she likes chicken

## her / his

pronoun.

Her / His + **noun** + verb.

#### Example

Her dog.

His chicken

His dog is cute.


## Summary

he / she plus verb. 

her/his plus noun.
